Who is Larry?

Guitarist & Audio Professional

Larry Seyer is a musician and audio professional with over 50 years of experience across virtually every music genre. He recognized his musical calling early in life and has pursued it with exceptional dedication ever since.

▲

Professional Background

Larry's career encompasses multiple roles: recording engineer, mixer, mastering engineer, guitarist, performer, and author. He applies a passion seldom seen within circles of his peers to whatever artistic medium he undertakes.

His work spans decades and genres, from country and rock to electronic and ambient music. This vast experience informs the unique sonic textures he brings to Ra's Pyramid.

▲

Ra's Pyramid

Through his collaboration with Marta Waterman in Ra's Pyramid, Larry channels his lifetime of musical knowledge into creating mystical soundscapes that transport listeners to ancient Egyptian landscapes and beyond.
