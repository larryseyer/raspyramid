Why Ra's Pyramid?

The Origin Story

The artists met while producing Divine Cosmos Convergence conferences for David Wilcock (Marta's son). Larry had collaborated with David since 2005 on music and web projects, including "Science of Peace" and "Wanderer Awakening."

During global conference events, Larry and David performed together, with Marta joining on keyboards. Their successful collaboration inspired them to form their own duo.

A Tribute to the Beginning

The name "Ra's Pyramid" honors David Wilcock's work and represents their tribute to the initial collaboration that brought them together. It evokes the ancient mysticism of Egypt and the timeless wisdom of Raâ€”themes that resonate deeply in their music.

Through Ra's Pyramid, Larry and Marta channel their shared spiritual connection into sonic landscapes that transport listeners to distant worlds and ancient times.
