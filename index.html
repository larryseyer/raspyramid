Welcome to Ra's Pyramid

Ra's Pyramid is a musical duo featuring Larry Seyer on guitar and Marta Waterman on synthesizer. Together, they create a magical, musical journey through the land of exotic, mystical places.

Using modern instruments, Ra's Pyramid crafts sonic landscapes from distant worlds—inviting listeners to experience a modern day ride across the cool desert sands of Egypt during a period ruled by Ra.

Larry Seyer

A musician and audio professional with over 50 years of experience across virtually every music genre. His career encompasses recording, mixing, mastering, performance, and writing—all pursued with a passion seldom seen within circles of his peers.

Marta Waterman

A musician, college professor, author, and personal trainer who has been performing since high school. She played piano by ear at age two and was reading music by age four. She performs across diverse genres including classical, metal, pop, and jazz.
