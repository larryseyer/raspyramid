Who is Marta?

Musician, Author & Synthesizer Artist

Marta Waterman is a musician, college professor, author, and personal trainer who has been performing since high school. Her musical journey began remarkably early—she played piano by ear at age two and was reading music by age four.

Musical Career

Marta performs across diverse genres including classical, metal, pop, and jazz, both as a soloist and in ensembles. Currently, she serves as keyboardist for the band Lunar Figurine.

She also creates original, improvised piano scores for the Rosendale Theatre's silent film series—a unique art form that combines her musical spontaneity with visual storytelling.

Published Works

Beyond music, Marta is an accomplished author. Her published works include:

  • The Life and Art of Robert J. Adsit
  • Dream, Believe, Create: How To Be a Life Magician

Ra's Pyramid

Marta's connection to spirit and the joyful, spontaneous synergy of artistic collaboration with Larry Seyer gives Ra's Pyramid its unique mystical quality. Her synthesizer work creates the ethereal soundscapes that define the duo's sonic identity.
